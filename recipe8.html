Bun Kebab Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 500g minced beef
  • 4 burger buns
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for frying

Nutritional Information:

Calories:
30cal
Protein:
04gm
Fat:
02gm

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix minced beef with chopped onions, green chilies, ginger-garlic paste, cumin seeds, coriander powder, red chili powder, turmeric powder, garam masala, and salt.
  2. Divide the mixture into equal portions and shape them into flat round patties.
  3. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Once hot, fry the kebab patties until golden brown and cooked through.
  4. Slice the burger buns horizontally and lightly toast them.
  5. Place a kebab patty between each bun and serve hot with your favorite chutney or sauce.
