Paya Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 4 cow or goat trotters
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch ginger, grated
  • 2 teaspoons garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/4 cup oil or ghee
  • Salt to taste

Nutritional Information:

Calories:
50cal
Protein:
40gm
Fat:
10gm

Instructions:

  1. Clean and wash the trotters thoroughly.
  2. In a large pot, heat oil or ghee over medium heat.
  3. Add sliced onions, minced garlic, and grated ginger. Saut√© until golden brown.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn soft and mushy.
  5. Add garam masala, red chili powder, turmeric powder, and salt. Mix well.
  6. Add the cleaned trotters to the pot and stir to coat them with the spice mixture.
  7. Add enough water to cover the trotters completely.
  8. Cover the pot and let it simmer on low heat for 4-5 hours, or until the trotters are tender and the gravy thickens.
  9. Once done, garnish with chopped coriander leaves and serve hot with naan or rice.
