Nihari Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg beef or mutton, cut into pieces
  • 2 onions, thinly sliced
  • 4 tablespoons Nihari masala
  • 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 cup wheat flour (for thickening)
  • 1/4 cup oil or ghee
  • Salt to taste
  • Chopped coriander leaves for garnish
  • Lemon wedges for serving

Nutritional Information:

Calories:
30cal
Protein:
14gm
Fat:
04gm

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, heat oil or ghee over medium heat.
  2. Add thinly sliced onions and cook until they turn golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and saut√© until the raw smell disappears.
  4. Add beef or mutton pieces and cook until they are browned on all sides.
  5. Add Nihari masala and salt. Stir well to coat the meat with the spices.
  6. Add enough water to cover the meat and bring it to a boil.
  7. Once boiling, reduce the heat to low, cover the pot, and let it simmer for 2-3 hours, or until the meat is tender.
  8. In a small bowl, mix wheat flour with some water to make a smooth paste.
  9. Add this paste to the pot and stir well to thicken the gravy.
  10. Simmer for another 30 minutes until the gravy reaches your desired consistency.
  11. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and serve hot with naan or rice, accompanied by lemon wedges.
