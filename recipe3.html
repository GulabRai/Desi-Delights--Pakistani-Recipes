Sarson Da Saag Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 500g mustard greens (sarson)
  • 250g spinach leaves
  • 2 green chilies, chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch ginger, grated
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 cup ghee
  • Salt to taste

Nutritional Information:

Calories:
25cal
Protein:
12gm
Fat:
01gm

Instructions:

  1. Thoroughly wash the mustard greens and spinach leaves.
  2. Finely chop the greens, onion, tomatoes, green chilies, garlic, and ginger.
  3. In a large pot, heat ghee over medium heat.
  4. Add cumin seeds and allow them to splutter.
  5. Add chopped garlic and ginger, and saut√© until golden brown.
  6. Add chopped onion and cook until translucent.
  7. Add chopped tomatoes and green chilies, and cook until tomatoes are soft.
  8. Add mustard greens and spinach leaves to the pot. Cook until wilted.
  9. Add red chili powder and salt to taste. Mix well.
  10. Mash the cooked greens using a potato masher or immersion blender until desired consistency is achieved.
  11. Cook the saag for another 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  12. Once done, serve hot with makki di roti (cornbread) and a dollop of butter.
