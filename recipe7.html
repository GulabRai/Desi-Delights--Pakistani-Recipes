Beef Korma Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg beef, cut into pieces
  • 2 onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 cup fried onions
  • 1/4 cup oil or ghee
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • Chopped coriander leaves for garnish
  • Ginger julienne for garnish
  • Salt to taste

Nutritional Information:

Calories:
20cal
Protein:
12gm
Fat:
04gm

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, heat oil or ghee over medium heat.
  2. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  3. Add thinly sliced onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook until the raw smell disappears.
  5. Add beef pieces and brown them on all sides.
  6. Once the beef is browned, add fried onions, cumin seeds, coriander powder, turmeric powder, and salt. Mix well.
  7. Whisk yogurt and add it to the pot. Stir continuously to avoid curdling.
  8. Cover the pot and let the beef cook on low heat until tender, stirring occasionally.
  9. Add garam masala and adjust salt if needed. Cook for an additional 5-10 minutes.
  10. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and ginger julienne.
  11. Serve hot with rice or naan.
