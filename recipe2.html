Chapli Kebab Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 500g minced beef or lamb
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 2 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili flakes
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 egg

Nutritional Information:

Calories:
40cal
Protein:
10gm
Fat:
12gm

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the ingredients by finely chopping the onion, tomatoes, and green chilies.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine minced beef or lamb with chopped onion, tomatoes, green chilies, ginger-garlic paste, coriander powder, cumin powder, red chili flakes, garam masala, and beaten egg. Mix well.
  3. Form small patties with the mixture and flatten them to a round shape.
  4. Heat oil in a frying pan over medium heat.
  5. Carefully place the chapli kebabs in the hot oil and fry until they are golden brown and cooked through, turning them once or twice during cooking.
  6. Once cooked, remove the kebabs from the pan and drain excess oil on paper towels.
  7. Serve hot with naan bread, mint chutney, and sliced onions.
