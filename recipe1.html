Haleem Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 500g boneless chicken or mutton
  • 1 cup wheat
  • 1/2 cup barley
  • 1/2 cup lentils (moong dal)
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 teaspoons garam masala
  • 1/4 cup fried onions

Nutritional Information:

Calories:
20cal
Protein:
20gm
Fat:
01gm

Instructions:

  1. Rinse and soak wheat grains and barley overnight.
  2. Cook meat with onions, garlic, ginger, and spices until tender.
  3. Add soaked lentils and grains to the pot with the cooked meat.
  4. Cook everything together until grains and lentils are soft and mushy.
  5. Blend the mixture until smooth, adjust seasoning, and add water if needed.
  6. Prepare a tempering of fried onions and pour over the haleem.
  7. Garnish with coriander leaves and sliced ginger, then serve hot with naan or rice.
